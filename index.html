📘 was / were U11
Sujeto✅ Afirmativo❌ Negativo
Iwaswasn't
he / she / itwaswasn't
you / we / theywereweren't
Pregunta yes/no: Was she home? → Yes, she was. / No, she wasn't.
Were they noisy? → Yes, they were. / No, they weren't.
Pregunta de información:
What color were the walls? → They were orange.
How old was he? → He was eight.
Where were you? → I was at home.
⚠️ Con adjetivos en pasado: He was cute. ✅   He cute. ❌
Del libro:
• I was eight. It was exciting!
• The walls were orange.
• My parents weren't there.
• It wasn't old.
